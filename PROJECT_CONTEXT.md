# Adobe Stock Intelligence Project

Goal:
Build a data-driven market research system for Adobe Stock contributors.

Core strategy:
Do not generate random beautiful images. Build commercial marketing assets based on buyer needs.

Core framework:
BUY Framework:
- Buyer: Who buys this image?
- Use Case: What will they use it for?
- Why Buy: Why would they buy this instead of another stock image?

Main direction:
Portfolio should become Commercial Marketing Asset Collections, not generic AI backgrounds.

Avoid:
- Luxury product podium
- Generic cosmetic background
- Floating serum
- Abstract product stages

Prioritize:
- Healthcare marketing environment
- Pet product marketing environment
- Retail packaging display shelf
- Frozen food freezer display
- Coffee packaging retail shelf

Scoring logic:
Opportunity Score should consider:
- Demand
- Competition
- Commercial Value
- Evergreen
- Reusability
- AI Saturation

Next product:
Build a Market Research Agent that analyzes niches and outputs structured rows for the Excel/Google Sheets database.
